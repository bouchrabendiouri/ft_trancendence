How to Play Ping Pong

Game Instructions

1. Serving

To serve, toss the ball up and hit it so it bounces on your side and then crosses the net.

2. Moving the Paddle

Use the arrow keys (left and right) to move your paddle and return the ball.

3. Scoring

Score when the opponent misses the ball. The game is played to 11 points.
